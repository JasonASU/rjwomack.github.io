Safety Requirements

Age Requirement

Drivers must be at least 18 years old with a valid driver's license. Passengers must be at least 6 years old and accompanied by an adult.

Safety Briefing

All renters must complete a mandatory safety briefing before operating a jet ski. This includes operating instructions, local waterway rules, and emergency procedures.

Life Jackets

USCG-approved life jackets are required at all times while on the water. We provide all necessary safety equipment at no additional charge.

Swimming Ability

All participants should be comfortable in water. While life jackets are provided, basic swimming skills are recommended for your safety and confidence.

Weather Conditions

We monitor weather conditions closely. Rentals may be cancelled or postponed due to high winds, storms, or unsafe water conditions.

Liability Waiver

All participants must sign a liability waiver before rental. Minors require parent or guardian signature. Security deposit required.
